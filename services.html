MY SERVICES


I support women with autoimmune and chronic conditions.
When you work with me you’ll learn not only lifestyle & nutrition strategies, but also right supplementation, that will help you to manage your condition in a holistic way.
All my programs will assist you to uncover food allergies and sensitivities and get to the root of your discomfort, rebuilt your gut and improve your health with pleasurable delicious food and lifestyle that fits your needs and busy life.
No one diet works for everyone. I’ll guide you to find the food and lifestyle choices that best support YOU. I’ll also help you to make GRADUAL changes that enable you to reach your current and future health goals.


My programs are for you if:

• You’re ready to take responsibility for yourself and for the choices you make in this lifetime
• You want someone to encourage you and hold you accountable to being your best self
• You want to live a complete life from a place of inspiration rather than desperation
• You’re willing to explore which habits are supporting you, and which ones are keeping you tired and drained
• You’d like to clean up areas in your lifestyle that you know aren’t perfect and are blocking you to live your life healthy and strong.


They’re NOT for you if:

• You’re not ready to invest in yourself
• You prefer being a victim
• You don’t want to dig deep and work hard to find out what’s possible and what has to be changed to achieve the life you deserve.


You’ll learn to…

• Eat foods that give you the most energy.
• Control cravings naturally.
• Manage weight without dieting.
• Harness the power of food and lifestyle choices to reach your goals.
• Reduce stress.
• Restore your digestive system.
• Make food fun, guilt-free! And allergy free
• Make easy, quick, delicious and nutritious meals All one on one programs can be bi-weekly if desired.
