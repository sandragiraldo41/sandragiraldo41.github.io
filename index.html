About me

Every day I help people live their best, healthiest life. I am a Certified Integrative Nutrition Health Coach and Detox Specialist. I support women with autoimmune and chronic conditions to utilize the power of nature specially food and transform their lives thriving in a body that loves them back.
My journey started 28 years ago when I was a geology student, my body started breaking down slowly, I had constant digestive problems, extreme fatigue and eczema. I got an IBS diagnose and a month later hypoglycemia was added to the list. After many doctors, diagnosis and living over medicated I didn’t get any better, fifteen years ago I got in charge, stopped feeling bad for myself and started my recovery journey, a journey that took me back to school and has allowed me to help other women just like me. Food has been my medicine and I’m sure it’ll be yours too.
